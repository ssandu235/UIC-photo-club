Who We Are

More Than a Club.
A Creative Home.

The Photography Student Club at UIC brings together students who share a passion for visual storytelling. Whether you shoot on film or digital, landscapes or portraits, we're here to learn, grow, and create together.

We host weekly photo walks, workshops on editing and technique, guest speaker events, and semester showcase exhibitions. All skill levels welcome — from first-time shooters to seasoned photographers.
